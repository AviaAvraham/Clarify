Privacy Policy for Clarify

Last update: April 23, 2025

1. Information We Collect

Clarify is designed to work entirely without collecting personal or device data. We do not collect, store, or share any personally identifiable information (PII).

Note: When you highlight a word, only that word (and minimal metadata required to process it) is sent to our external service. No user identifiers or device details accompany it.

2. How We Use Your Information

Because we don’t collect personal data, there’s nothing to use for advertising, profiling, or any other purpose. Highlighted words are used solely to fetch definitions or clarifications and are not retained beyond the immediate request.

3. No Tracking or Advertising

Clarify contains no advertising SDKs, analytics trackers, or cookies. We do not track your usage across apps or services. Your lookup habits remain private.

4. Third-Party Services

To provide word definitions, Clarify relies on external dictionary/NLP APIs. These services receive only the highlighted word and return its meaning. We have chosen partners that:

  • Do not store or link words to individual users.
  • Operate under strict privacy standards.

5. Data Storage and Retention

Since we collect no personal data:

  • Nothing is stored on our servers or your device beyond the app’s default cache.
  • No logs of highlighted words are retained.

6. Your Rights

  • Use Anonymously: No account or signup is required.
  • Control Over Data: Because we collect no PII, you inherently control your data—and there is none to delete.

7. Children’s Privacy

Clarify is suitable for all ages. As we don’t collect any personal information, there are no special privacy concerns for users under 13. If children use the service, no parental consent is required because no data is gathered.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect service improvements or legal requirements. If we make material changes, we will note the update date here and, where feasible, notify you via an in-app notice or update note.
